How I got here

My journey started with a B.Tech in Computer Science from Delhi University (Top 20% of batch).

I went on to complete an M.Tech from Punjab Engineering College with a 9.61 CGPA, ranked 2nd in class, and served as Placement Head — building the coordination and communication skills that define my leadership style today.

A data engineering internship at Snapdeal gave me early hands-on experience with large-scale ETL and customer behaviour analytics. Shortly after, I joined McKinsey & Company in 2021 — where I've been building and leading ever since.

At McKinsey, I grew from delivering pipelines to leading a team of 7+ data engineers, spanning domains of Manufacturing, Supply Chain, and Capital Projects. My cloud journey expanded from Azure mastery into GCP and MS Fabric — each transition backed by certifications and real production deployments. I’ve layered in Gen AI: LLM agents for recommendations, code review, docs, and source–target validation, using tools like Cursor and ChatGPT.
